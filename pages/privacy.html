Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 14, 2026 Applies to: DailyKit web app and installed PWA

This Privacy Policy explains how DailyKit handles information when you use the app. DailyKit is designed as an offline-first personal tracker. In the current public release, users access the app through guest mode and data is stored locally on the device unless the user chooses to export a backup file.

What data DailyKit stores

DailyKit may store the following categories of user-created data on your device:

  • Expense entries, including item name, amount, date, and category
  • Borrowed money entries, including person name, amount, and date
  • Grocery list entries
  • Language preference and local session identifier
  • Locally exported backup files if you choose to create them

How data is stored

DailyKit currently stores user data locally in your browser using device-side storage technologies such as Local Storage and cached app assets for offline use. DailyKit is built so that the app can continue working without an internet connection.

Cloud sync and third-party services

At the time of this policy, the public release of DailyKit uses guest mode by default and does not require account creation to use core features. Optional third-party sign-in or cloud backup features may be introduced later. If those features are activated in a future release, this policy should be updated before relying on those services.

Backups and user control

You can export a backup of your DailyKit data manually. You are responsible for storing exported files securely and for deciding where those files are saved or shared. You can also delete entries directly inside the app at any time.

Cookies and analytics

DailyKit is not intended to rely on advertising trackers. Basic browser caching and related app functionality may be used to support offline access, performance, and installation behavior.

Children's privacy

DailyKit is not directed to children under 13, and it is not intended for use where special legal protections for children's data would apply without additional compliance steps.

Security

Reasonable efforts may be made to protect the app and its locally stored data, but no storage or transmission method is guaranteed to be completely secure. You should avoid storing highly sensitive personal, financial, medical, legal, or government identification information in DailyKit.

Professional-use disclaimer

DailyKit is a personal organization tool. It does not provide financial, tax, accounting, medical, legal, or other professional advice, and it should not be relied on as a substitute for professional services.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as DailyKit evolves. When material changes are made, the operator should update the effective date and publish the revised policy before relying on new data practices.
