Install Help

Install DailyKit on iPhone or iPad

1. Open in Safari If you are using Chrome or another iPhone browser, open DailyKit in Safari first.
2. Tap the Share button Use the Share icon in Safari to open the action sheet.
3. Choose Add to Home Screen After that, DailyKit can launch in standalone mode from your home screen.
